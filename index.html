Our Community

A Scenic, Peaceful Place to Call Home

Family owned and in operation since 1960, Lake View Terrace is a proud 55+ age resident community of manufactured homes and seasonal RV sites set in the heart of the Hudson Valley.

Our park offers a scenic and peaceful living environment where neighbors know each other and tranquility is part of daily life. Whether you're looking for a permanent manufactured home or a seasonal RV getaway, we'd love to welcome you.
